## Illustrative soil profile and infrastructure cases

The three-layer profile and the building, bridge and equipment scenarios are deliberately labelled illustrative. The central values are retained only to demonstrate the screening workflow. The uncertainty analysis varies `mv` by 0.6–1.4, `cv` by 0.3–3.0 on a logarithmic scale, `Lambda` by 0.6–1.4, temperature by ±20%, load and capacity by ±10%, and vertical stiffness by 0.7–1.3. Site application requires measured or project-derived values.
